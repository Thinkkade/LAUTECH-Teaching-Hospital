Prof. Oladimeji George OPADIJO
Chief Medical Director

The Chief Medical Director is the Hospital's Chief Executive and in that capacity has responsibility for the day-to-day management of the entire hospital under the broad direction of the Board of Management. The Chief Medical Director in addition direct responsibility for key Departments like Accounts and Finance, Internal Audit, Information and Communication Technology Unit (ICT), Public Relations,Engineering, Planning, Security Services. Stores & Supplies, Staff development, as well as relationship with external bodies. The statutory role of the Chief Medical Director is that of an over-all coordinator of the entire functions of the hospital. Each Director may be ascribed specific responsibility for a group of activities, The Chief Medical Director has global responsibility even for the functions assigned to the various directors.

Dr. Michael Olaniyan ONIGBINDE
Chairman Medical Advisory Commitee

The office of the Chairman, Medical Advisory Committee coordinates the actitivies of the hospital's training schools/programmes and the clinical departments. The office ensures that the conditions set up by the bodies respnsible for the certification of paramedical personnel trained by the hospital are met. It handles the training condition of resident doctors with the active assistance/collaboration of the Chairman,Postgraduate Medical Training Committee. It is the duty of the Chairman, Medical advisory Committee's office to ensure that all patients visiting the hospital are given adequate, prompt and proper attention. It serves as the link between the hospital and the outside world on clinical, training and patient-care matters. The Directorate of Clinical Services and Training is the administrative arm headed by the Chairman, Medical Advisory Committee. The Directorate oversees all clinical services and training rendered by the hospital and also supervises all clinical departments/units.

Alhaji Mukaila Ademola AJIFERUKE
Director of Administration

The Director of Administration is the head of the Directorate of Administration at the Lautech University Teaching Hospitals, Ogbomoso. There are two (2) directorate in the Teaching Hospital namely: Directorate of Administration headed by the Director of Administration and Directorate of Clinical services and training headed by the Chairman, Medical Adversory Committee. The two directorate are accountable to the Chief Medical director. The director of Administration is the secretary to the management Board of the Teaching Hospital. The management Board is a statutory body appointed by theFederal Government for the overall supervision and policy formulation of the teaching hospital. The office of the Director of Administration has the underlisted non-clinical departments/units under it through which the office carries out its functions. Establishments/Human Resources management, General Administration incorporating Procurement Unit, Accounts & Finance, Planning & Development, Stores & Supplies Catering, Health Information Management, Security Unit, Hospital Unit Administration, Maintenance Department including Biomedical, Electrical, Civil and Mechanical engineering units.
