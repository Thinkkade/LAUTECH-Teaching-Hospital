In one week, LTH Ogbomoso performed two limb salvage surgeries.

  • LTH
  • October 24, 2016
  • News
  • 5 Comments

Our team of experts led by Dr Onilede, a plastic surgeon, performed two limb salvage surgeries in one week. One was a 40 year old man who sustained a left open tibio-fibular fracture (Gustillo-Abderson type IIIC) in a motorcycle crash. He was resuscitated and subsequently had the fracture reduced and immobilized with an external fixator and the posterior tibial artery repaired microsurgically by a team of experts. The second patient was a 60 year old right handed man who presented with right ulna fracture and multiple wounds with associated radial and ulnar artery injuries. The patient was resuscitated and had a successful vascular repair. The significance of these interventions is that both patients would have ended with amputation of the involved limbs with the attendant socio-economic implications. There might even be loss of lives. The first picture: the repaired left posterior tibial artery using a venous graft The second picture: end to end anastomosis of ulnar artery

Two of our Doctors from Gynaecology department, Dr. Lanre Bajowa and Dr. Oluseyi Atanda, who are resident and consultant respectively have been successfully trained on Gynaecological Laparoscopy in a world class hospital in China. Xiangyang No.1 People’s Hospital, Xiangyang Hospital Affiliated to Hubei University of Medicine, was founded On August 1, 1949. It is a Class A tertiary comprehensive hospital that contains medical, education, prevention and health care. It has won numerous honors, Honored With Model Establishment in Hubei Province, Advanced Hospital Culture Construction Unit, China’s Best Units of Spiritual Civilization, Greatly Trusted Model Hospital. The hospital comprises 51 ward areas and 40 specialist departments with a total of 2050 sickbeds. There are 2400 sickbeds actually. The indices amounts to 1 million outpatient , 70000 discharged patients and 20000 surgeries. Among its 2342 staff members are a great number of experts and scholars, including 1838 professionals, 62 holders of Senior professional title, 213 holders of Sub-senior professional title, 314 masters and doctors, 18 postgraduate tutors, 12 holders of special government allowances from China's State Council, 61 High-level talents of the New Century in Hubei province, 12 Xiangyang Longzhong Famous Doctors. The hospital formed a reasonable personnel structure of discipline groups with high, medium and primary talents. On behalf of the management and the entire hospital staff, Drs Atanda and Bajowa are dearly welcome back to Nigeria.
