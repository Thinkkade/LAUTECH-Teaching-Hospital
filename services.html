Chemical Pathology

Chemical Pathology Department comprises of three units: 1. Metabolic unit 2. Immunology unit 3. Routine Chemistry analysis unit for easy functions. It sub serve functions like teaching, service and research within and outside the institution. The Department trains IT students, students of Medical Laboratory Science, Medical Students and Resident Doctors.

The Department does analysis, report and gives detailed pathologic interpretations to most chemistry and immunology assays such as renal function tests, liver function tests, bone panel, fertility tests, tumor markers, thyroid function tests, various dynamic tests and other routine biochemical analysis. The metabolic unit comprises of a functional Obesity and investigative clinics, an observatory ward, as well as a functional Metabolic research laboratory (MRL). The Department has taken part in many researches both local and international.

Internal Medicine

The department of medicine is fully equipped to serve the population. The world class dialysis centre with eight dialysis machine is a state of the art comparable to any other in the world. The Cardiology unit also offers full inpatient cardiovascular services. Other units offering specialty service include Neurology, Gastroenterology, Dermatology and Respiratory Medicine. With latest state of the art equipment including echocardiography, electrocardiography, electroencephalography, endoscopy, glycated haemoglobin machine among others, the department offers quality service via specialist and general outpatient medical clinic and 60 bedded male and female wards for inpatient service.

Haematology

The department of haematology and blood transfusion fully commenced operations in 2011 and currently has a staff complement comprising of 3 consultants, 5 resident doctors, 11 medical laboratory scientists, 5 laboratory technicians, 8 lab attendants and 3 administrative staff. We service the needs of the hospital’s in-patient and out-patient populations, providing a wide range of laboratory and clinical consultation.

It is our vision to be a referral centre of repute for the investigation and management of haematological disorders with a corporate commitment to excellence of service and social responsibility using all the knowledge and skills at our disposal for the service and dignity of humanity, while achieving our individual and collective professional aspirations.

Surgery

The department of surgery started operating right from the onset of the opening of the hospital in 2011. The department has the following units general surgery, urology, orthopaedics, Burns and plastic, paediatric surgery, neurosurgery and cardiothoracic units. The department has 15 consultants, 4 senior registrars and 13 registrars. Our mission is to serve the community in surgical capacity at tertiary level and our vision is to have all the sophiscated equipments that is needed to solve the complicated surgical problems that drive our people into medical tourism.

Mental Health

The mental health department provides in-patient and outpatient care to persons with mental and substance use disorders. It also offers 24-hour emergency care and receives referrals from neighbouring communities.Our clinical and research interests include Addiction, Child and adolescent, Consultation liaison and Social psychiatry.The mental health team of psychiatrists, psychologists, and nurses, occupational therapists and social workers delivers evidence based therapeutic services including ECT and psychotherapy. Our residency program is fully accredited by the National Postgraduate Medical College of Nigeria (NPMCN) and West African College of Psychiatry (WACP).

Microbiology

Department of Microbiology as a part of the Pathology provides diagnostic services, research and training programs focused on infectious diseases. -The Department is involved in the diagnosis and management of diseases caused by micro-organisms (bacteria, fungi, viruses, protozoa and parasites). -We provide timely and high quality diagnostic and advisory service to patients and local community. -The diagnostic service we provide includes testing clinical specimens such as urine, blood, tissues and swabs, for micro-organisms and other evidence of infection in: Bacteriology including Chlamydia screening service Mycology, Serology, Virology, Parasitology, Seminology

Microbiology

-Our specialist staff provide comprehensive clinical services. Clinical microbiology advice is provided regarding all aspects of testing, treatment, management and prevention of infectious diseases. The Department also holds a sexually transmitted infection clinics, see in-patients on the wards by request and works closely with the Hospital Infection Control team. -There is a 24 hour out of hours, on-call service available for both urgent specimen processing and clinical advice.

Cervical Cancer - Symptoms and Signs

Any of the following could be signs or symptoms of cervical dysplasia or cancer: 1. Blood spots or light bleeding between or following periods 2. Menstrual bleeding that is longer and heavier than usual 3. Bleeding after intercourse, douching, or a pelvic examination 4. Pain during sexual intercourse 5. Bleeding after menopause 6. Increased vaginal discharge

How to prevent Glaucoma

1. Get regular eye care. 2. Regular comprehensive eye exams can help detect glaucoma in its early stages before irreversible damage occurs. 3. Know your family's eye health history. Glaucoma tends to run in families. 4. Exercise safely. 5. Take prescribed eyedrops regularly. 6. Wear eye protection.
